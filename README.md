## ğŸ’¡ How to Play

1. The board positions are numbered **1 to 9**:

```
1 | 2 | 3
---------
4 | 5 | 6
---------
7 | 8 | 9
```

2. Player X always goes first.  
3. Players take turns entering the number corresponding to the board position.  
4. The game automatically checks for **win**, **draw**, or **invalid moves**.  
5. The first player to align **three marks in a row, column, or diagonal** wins.
